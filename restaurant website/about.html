Our History

“Established in 1995, our restaurant started as a small family-owned business in the heart of Borivali. Over the years, we have grown into
a beloved local institution, known for our authentic cuisine and warm hospitality. Our journey has been marked by dedication
to quality, a passion for food, and a commitment to our community. Today, we are proud to serve generations of patrons
who have become a part of our extended family.”
